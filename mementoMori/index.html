Memento Mori

Remember that You Die

This project is based on the story by Han Gyu Lee, published on the Daily Tomorrow(2021).

Part 1. When I was Young

When I was seven, my dad asked me to get a bottle of water from a well in our neighborhood.
I went to the well and tried to fill water to my bottle - but the water was too far from my short arms.
I slowly put myself into the well so that my bottle get close to water, but suddenly I fell into the well.

I thought I was dying that day.
But then my friend's dad who was passing by ran into and rescued me.
That was the first experience of facing that, that I remember.

I faced death several times since I was young.
When I was in high school, I got gas poioning from coal briquettes while sleeping.
I lost my consciousness, and brought to a hospital.
Since then, I seriously thought about my life, spirit and death.

Part 2. What if I Disappear Eternally?

It was scary to face death alon in a moment that I cannot expect.
I was filled with anguish, because I didn't know what would happen to my spirit after death.
I read many religious books and listened to the speeches from religious leaders expecting that they would give me an answer.
However, those did not provide any answers to me.
