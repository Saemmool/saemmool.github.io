How Covid-19 Spread in South Korea for the Past Two Years

By Saemmool Lee April 2, 2022

South Korea was once touted as a successful model in managing Covid-19. However, the country got the highest number of daily cases up to 600,000 in March 2022 in the world. How the virus spread in the country during the pandemic?

The first case at Incheon

in January 10, 2020

Five months after mass infection in Daegu

It started with one person. The government found an infected case while inspecting people entering from Wuhan at the airport.

There were more cases in Southern region where mass infection occurred in the early phase.

Korean government tightened social distancing to control the virus, such as restricting private gathering more than five people. However, the virus spread all over the region.

Coronavirus spread massively around Seoul, where almost half of the population of the country live.

The cases got even bigger. The cases went up as high as 600,000 a day. The virus was uncontrollable.

The government loosened social distancing as time goes by. Many people got tired of the restrictions - some suffered eonomic loss due to the regulations.

After the Big Wave
