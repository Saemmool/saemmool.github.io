Saemmool Lee is a media strategist, journalist, and book writer.

She received her master's degree from UC Berkeley Graduate School of Journalism('19) with concentration on new media, and undergraduate degree in Journalism and Mass Communication from Korea University('12).

Her main interest is in intersection of data science, computational journalism, and media industry. She has an extensive career in journalism from traditional publications to online media, and from newsroom to strategy department.
