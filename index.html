About

Saemmool Lee is a media innovator based in Seoul, South Korea. She currently works as a staff reporter at the Dong-A Ilbo in Seoul, South Korea, where she works at the corporate strategy unit.

She received her master's degree from UC Berkeley Graduate School of Journalism, and undergraduate degree in Journalism and Mass Communication from Korea University.

Her main interest is in intersection of data science and media industry. Though she focuses on innovating online journalism, she has a strong background in print publications with her career as a newspaper reporter and book writer.
