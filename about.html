Saemmool Lee is a journalist focusing on the future of media.

She started her career as a beat reporter at the Dong-A Ilbo, one of the biggest and oldest daily newspapers in Seoul, covering a wide range of beats from crime to business for six years. Then she earned her master's degree from the UC Berkeley Graduate School of Journalism('19) with a concentration on new media. During her graduate studies, she worked at the Center for Information Technology Research in the Interest of Society (CITRIS) and the Banatao Institute communications team for a year. Currently, she is managing digital media strategies and for the Dong-A Media Group, and working on a business plan for its broadcasting company Channel A at the Dong-A Ilbo's corporate strategy team.

She has authored four books, which you can find more about here.
